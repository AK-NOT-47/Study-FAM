# Me Studing Stuff 🎓

A focused, modern study website. Right now it holds one exam: **SOA Exam FAM**.

## ▶ How to use it

**Double-click `index.html`** — that's it. No install, no server, no setup.
It opens in your browser as a full study site with notes, formula sheets, worked problems,
search (press **Ctrl/⌘ + K**), light/dark themes, and progress tracking.

> Tip: right-click `index.html` → *Open with* → your browser, then bookmark it.

## What's inside

- **Dashboard** — progress ring, exam countdown, and "jump back in."
- **Getting Started** — exam overview, a study plan, and resources.
- **FAM-L** (long-term / life contingencies) and **FAM-S** (short-term / loss models) topic notes.
- **Formula sheets**, **practice & worked examples**, and an interactive **Progress Tracker**.
