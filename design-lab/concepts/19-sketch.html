Life Insurance Present Values

The expected present value of a benefit paid on death — the heart of every FAM-L problem.

formulas ✦
Ax = 1 − d·äx
Var(Z) = ²Ax − (Ax)²
äx = (1 − Ax) / d
these three link up — memorise one, derive the rest!
⚠️ Common trap

Under UDD, multiply ONLY the term/death piece by i/δ — the pure endowment is never rescaled.
