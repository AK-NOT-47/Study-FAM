Life Insurance Present Values

The expected present value of a benefit, the building block behind every premium and reserve you'll compute on exam day.

A_x = 1 − d·ä_x · Var(Z) = ²A_x − (A_x)² · ä_x = (1 − A_x) / d

Common trap

Under UDD, multiply ONLY the term/death piece by i/δ — the pure endowment is never rescaled.
