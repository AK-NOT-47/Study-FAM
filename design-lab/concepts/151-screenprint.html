Life Insurance Present Values

The expected present value of a benefit paid on death — and how its variance falls right out of the same building blocks.

Ax = 1 − d·äx
Var(Z) = 2Ax − (Ax)2
äx = (1 − Ax) / d
⚠ Common Trap

Under UDD, multiply ONLY the term/death piece by i/δ — the pure endowment is never rescaled.
