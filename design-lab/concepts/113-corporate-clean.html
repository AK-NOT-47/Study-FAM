Life Insurance

The actuarial present value of a benefit paid on death — and the variance you'll be asked to compute under a constant force or UDD assumption.

Core identities
1 Ax = 1 − d·äx
2 Var(Z) = 2Ax − (Ax)2
3 äx = (1 − Ax) / d
⚠️
Common trap

Under UDD, multiply ONLY the term/death piece by i/δ — the pure endowment is never rescaled.
