Life Insurance Present Values

The actuarial present value of a benefit paid on death — the seed from which premiums, reserves and variance all grow.

EPV Ax = 1 − d·äx
Variance Var(Z) = 2Ax − (Ax)²
Annuity äx = (1 − Ax) / d
🐛
Common trap

Under UDD, multiply ONLY the term/death piece by i/δ — the pure endowment is never rescaled.
