Life Insurance Present Values

The actuarial present value of a benefit paid at death — and the spread of that random outcome.

FORMULA SHEETEPV · Var
i.A_x = 1 − d·ä_x
ii.Var(Z) = 2A_x − (A_x)2
iii.ä_x = (1 − A_x) / d
Common trap Under UDD, multiply ONLY the term/death piece by i/δ — the pure endowment is never rescaled.
