Life Insurance Present Values

The expected present value of a benefit paid on death — the trunk every other formula branches from.

EPV Ax = 1 − d·äx
Var Var(Z) = ²Ax − (Ax)²
Annuity äx = (1 − Ax) / d
🍄
Common trap

Under UDD, multiply ONLY the term/death piece by i/δ — the pure endowment is never rescaled.
