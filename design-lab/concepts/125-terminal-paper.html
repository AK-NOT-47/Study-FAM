Life Insurance Present Values
EPV of a benefit paid on death, and the variance of its present-value random variable.
key identities
01Ax = 1 − d·äx
02Var(Z) = 2Ax − (Ax)2
03äx = (1 − Ax) / d
⚠
Common trap

Under UDD, multiply ONLY the term/death piece by i/δ — the pure endowment is never rescaled.
