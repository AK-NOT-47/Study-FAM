Life Insurance Present Values

The expected present value of a benefit paid on death — the backbone of every insurance calculation.

Key relationships
  • 1A_x = 1 − d·ä_x
  • 2Var(Z) = ²A_x − (A_x)²
  • 3ä_x = (1 − A_x) / d
🪤
Common trap

Under UDD, multiply ONLY the term/death piece by i/δ — the pure endowment is never rescaled.
