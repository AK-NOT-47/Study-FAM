Life Insurance Present Values

The EPV of a benefit paid on death — tie it back to the annuity and you barely memorize anything.

A_x = 1 − d·ä_x
Var(Z) = ²A_x − (A_x)²
ä_x = (1 − A_x) / d

Under UDD, multiply ONLY the term/death piece by i/δ — the pure endowment is never rescaled.

Whole life EPV & variance

Set up A_x, grab ²A_x from the table, then slot both into Var(Z) = ²A_x − (A_x)². No calculus required — just clean bookkeeping.
