Life Insurance Present Values

The expected present value of a benefit paid on death — the backbone of every FAM-L insurance formula.

A Ax = 1 − d·äx
σ² Var(Z) = 2Ax − (Ax)2
ä äx = (1 − Ax) / d
⚠️ Common trap

Under UDD, multiply ONLY the term/death piece by i/δ — the pure endowment is never rescaled.
