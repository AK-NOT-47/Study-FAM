Life Insurance Present Values

The EPV of a benefit payable on death — and how its variance falls straight out of the second moment.

formulas/life-insurance.fx — phosphor
1# whole-life EPV via annuity
2A_x = 1 − d·ä_x
3# variance from 2nd moment
4Var(Z) = ²A_x − (A_x)²
5# annuity ↔ insurance bridge
6ä_x = (1 − A_x) / d
⚠Common trap · UDD

Under UDD, multiply only the term/death piece by i/δ — the pure endowment is never rescaled.
