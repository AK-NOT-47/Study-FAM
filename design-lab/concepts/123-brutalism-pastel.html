Topic Life Insurance Present Values

The expected present value of a benefit paid at death — and how its variance falls out of the second moment.

A_x = 1 − d·ä_x
Var(Z) = ²A_x − (A_x)²
ä_x = (1 − A_x) / d
Common trap

Under UDD, multiply ONLY the term/death piece by i/δ — the pure endowment is never rescaled.
