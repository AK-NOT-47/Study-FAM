Life Insurance Present Values

The expected present value of a benefit, and how its variance and the annuity identity all fall out of one relationship.

Key relations
01Ax = 1 − d·äx
02Var(Z) = 2Ax − (Ax)2
03äx = (1 − Ax) / d
⚠ Common trap
Under UDD, multiply ONLY the term/death piece by i/δ — the pure endowment is never rescaled.
