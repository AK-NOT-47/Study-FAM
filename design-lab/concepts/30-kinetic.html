Life Insurance Present Values

The expected present value of a benefit, and how its variance falls out of the same building blocks.

Whole life Ax = 1 − d·äx
Variance Var(Z) = 2Ax − (Ax)2
Annuity äx = (1 − Ax) / d
⚠️ Common trap

Under UDD, multiply ONLY the term/death piece by i/δ — the pure endowment is never rescaled.
