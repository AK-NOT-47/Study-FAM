Life Insurance Present Values

The expected present value (EPV) of a benefit paid on death — the backbone of every premium and reserve you'll compute on exam day.

A_x = 1 − d·ä_x discount link
Var(Z) = ²A_x − (A_x)² 2nd moment − mean²
ä_x = (1 − A_x) / d rearranged
⚠️
Common trap

Under UDD, multiply ONLY the term/death piece by i/δ — the pure endowment is never rescaled.
