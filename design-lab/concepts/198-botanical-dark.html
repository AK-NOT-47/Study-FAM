Life Insurance Present Values

The expected present value of a benefit, and how its variance falls right out of the second moment.

Ax = 1 − d·äxEPV ↔ annuity
Var(Z) = 2Ax − (Ax)2Variance
äx = (1 − Ax) / dAnnuity ↔ EPV
⚠ Common trap

Under UDD, multiply ONLY the term/death piece by i/δ — the pure endowment is never rescaled.
