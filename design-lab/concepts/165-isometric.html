Life Insurance Present Values

The EPV of a benefit, its variance, and the annuity link — the trio you reach for again and again.

EPV (whole life)A_x = 1 − d·ä_x
VarianceVar(Z) = ²A_x − (A_x)²
Annuity linkä_x = (1 − A_x) / d
⚠️ Common trap

Under UDD, multiply ONLY the term/death piece by i/δ — the pure endowment is never rescaled.
