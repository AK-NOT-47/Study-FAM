Life Insurance Present Values topic 03 · core formulas
FAM-L

The expected present value (EPV) of a benefit paid at death, plus its variance — the backbone of premium and reserve calculations.

formulas.fam life-insurance.md
1 A_x = 1 − d·ä_x
2 Var(Z) = ²A_x − (A_x)²
3 ä_x = (1 − A_x) / d
!
Common trap

Under UDD, multiply ONLY the term/death piece by i/δ — the pure endowment is never rescaled.
