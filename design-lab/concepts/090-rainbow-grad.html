Life Insurance Present Values

The relationships that tie whole-life EPV, its variance, and the annuity-due together — memorise the trio, win the marks.

Ax = 1 − d·äx
Var(Z) = 2Ax − (Ax)2
äx = (1 − Ax) / d

Common trap

Under UDD, multiply ONLY the term/death piece by i/δ — the pure endowment is never rescaled.
