🛡️ Life Insurance Present Values

Discounting future benefits to today — the EPV of a benefit paid on death, and how its variance falls out.

Ax = 1 − d·äx
Var(Z) = 2Ax − (Ax)2
äx = (1 − Ax) / d
⚠

Common trap

Under UDD, multiply ONLY the term/death piece by i/δ — the pure endowment is never rescaled.
