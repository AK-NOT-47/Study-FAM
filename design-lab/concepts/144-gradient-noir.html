Life Insurance Present Values

The EPV of a benefit, its variance, and how the annuity-insurance identity ties them together.

  key_identities.fam
01 A_x = 1 − d·ä_x
02 Var(Z) = ²A_x − (A_x)²
03 ä_x = (1 − A_x) / d
⚠️
Common trap

Under UDD, multiply ONLY the term/death piece by i/δ — the pure endowment is never rescaled.
