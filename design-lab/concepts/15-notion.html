🛡️ Life Insurance Present Values

Expected present value of a benefit paid on death — the core building block for every premium and reserve.

1Ax = 1 − d·äx⠿
2Var(Z) = 2Ax − (Ax)2⠿
3äx = (1 − Ax) / d⠿
⚠️
Common trap
Under UDD, multiply ONLY the term/death piece by i/δ — the pure endowment is never rescaled.
