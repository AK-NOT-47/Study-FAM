Life Insurance Present Values

The EPV of a benefit and its variance, tied to the annuity through the discount factor d — the three identities every FAM question leans on.

=E6 · EPV
Ax = 1 − d·äx
=G6 · VARIANCE
Var(Z) = ²Ax − (Ax)²
=J6 · ANNUITY
äx = (1 − Ax) / d
#TRAP! Common trap · UDD

Don't rescale the pure endowment.

Under UDD, multiply ONLY the term/death piece by i/δ — the pure endowment is never rescaled.
