Life Insurance Present Values

03 ·

The expected present value of a benefit paid on death — the backbone of pricing. Master these three identities and most of Life Insurance unlocks.

whole-life identities
EPV Ax = 1 − d·äx
VAR Var(Z) = ²Ax − (Ax)²
ANN äx = (1 − Ax) / d
⚠️
Common trap

Under UDD, multiply ONLY the term/death piece by i/δ — the pure endowment is never rescaled.
